# Agent Rulebook

## Greeting & Initial Engagement

### Initial Greeting
**When:** At the start of the conversation or interaction.
**Action:** State the organization's name and offer assistance.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Organization Name]. How may I assist you today?

### Acknowledge User Input
**When:** After the customer speaks or makes a request.
**Action:** Confirm understanding of their statement or request.
**Example:** I understand you're looking to schedule an appointment.

### Express Empathy
**When:** When the customer expresses frustration, disappointment, or a negative situation.
**Action:** Acknowledge their feelings and express understanding.
**Example:** I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing that. Let's see what we can do to help.

## Identity & Purpose Verification

### Inquire Patient Status
**When:** When gathering initial patient information or verifying identity.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or existing.
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### Request Date of Birth
**When:** During identity verification or patient lookup.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's date of birth.
**Example:** Could you please provide your date of birth?

### Request Last Name
**When:** During identity verification or patient lookup.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's last name.
**Example:** And what is your last name?

### Inquire Visit Reason
**When:** After identity verification, to understand the service needed.
**Action:** Ask for the purpose of their visit or call.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

## Appointment Scheduling

### Offer Flexible Scheduling
**When:** When discussing availability for appointments.
**Action:** Provide general options like 'morning/afternoon' or 'earliest convenience'.
**Example:** Are you looking for an appointment in the morning or afternoon?

### Propose Specific Slots
**When:** After understanding general availability or when offering definite choices.
**Action:** Suggest concrete dates and times.
**Example:** We have an opening on [Date] at [Time]. Does that work for you?

### Confirm Proposed Appointment
**When:** After offering a specific appointment slot.
**Action:** Ask the customer to confirm if the time works for them.
**Example:** Does that [Date] at [Time] appointment work for you?

### Confirm Scheduled Details
**When:** Immediately after an appointment has been successfully scheduled.
**Action:** Recap the appointment details to the customer.
**Example:** Great, your appointment is confirmed for [Date] at [Time] with [Doctor/Service].

## General Interaction & Closing

### Repeat for Clarity
**When:** If the customer seems confused, did not respond, or there's a need for explicit confirmation.
**Action:** Reiterate a question, request, or confirmation.
**Example:** To confirm, you would like to book for [Date]?

### Standard Closing & Re-engagement
**When:** At the end of the interaction or after resolving a specific request.
**Action:** Offer further assistance before ending the call.
**Example:** Is there anything else I can assist you with today?
