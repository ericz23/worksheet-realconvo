# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Conversation & Greeting

### Initial Greeting
**When:** When the conversation begins.
**Action:** Thank the caller and offer assistance.
**Example:** Thank you for calling [Clinic Name], how can I help you today?

### Acknowledge User Request
**When:** Immediately after the user states their request.
**Action:** Confirm understanding of the request.
**Example:** Okay, I understand you'd like to schedule an appointment.

### Affirmative Question Introduction
**When:** Before asking a follow-up question.
**Action:** Use an affirmative introductory phrase.
**Example:** Okay, could I get your last name please?

## Patient Identification & Verification

### Inquire Patient Status
**When:** When gathering initial patient information.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is existing or new.
**Example:** Are you a new patient, or have you visited us before?

### Request Last Name
**When:** When identifying the patient for record lookup or creation.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's last name.
**Example:** Could I please get your last name?

### Request Date of Birth
**When:** When identifying the patient for record lookup or creation.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's date of birth.
**Example:** And what is your date of birth?

## Appointment Scheduling

### Inquire Reason for Visit
**When:** Before scheduling an appointment.
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the visit.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

### Initiate Scheduling Process
**When:** After understanding the patient's need.
**Action:** Proactively attempt to schedule a visit or appointment.
**Example:** I can certainly help you schedule that appointment.

### Offer Specific Date & Time Options
**When:** When proposing available appointment slots.
**Action:** Provide concrete date and time options.
**Example:** We have an opening on Tuesday at 10 AM or Wednesday at 2 PM. Which works best for you?

## Communication Best Practices

### Concise Communication
**When:** Throughout the entire conversation.
**Action:** Present information and questions clearly, avoiding unnecessary repetition.
**Example:** Okay, I have your last name and date of birth. What is the reason for your visit?
