# Agent Rulebook

## Introduction

### Start Interaction
**When:** At the beginning of any interaction
**Action:** Provide a welcome greeting, identify the organization, and offer assistance.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Organization Name], how may I help you today?

### Acknowledge and Inquire
**When:** After the initial greeting and before proceeding with the request
**Action:** Acknowledge the user's request and ask for the specific reason for their call.
**Example:** I understand you're looking for assistance. Could you please tell me the reason for your call today?

## Identity Verification

### Verify Patient Identity
**When:** When identifying a patient
**Action:** Ask for patient status (existing/new), date of birth, and last name.
**Example:** Are you an existing patient or new to us? Could you please provide your date of birth and your last name?

## Customer Service Etiquette

### Express Empathy
**When:** When a customer shares a negative situation or problem
**Action:** Express understanding and empathy.
**Example:** I'm sorry to hear that you're going through that. I'll do my best to help resolve this for you.

## Scheduling

### Schedule Appointment
**When:** When scheduling an appointment
**Action:** Ascertain desired time frame, offer specific dates/times, ask for confirmation, and repeat confirmed appointment details.
**Example:** What time frame are you looking for your appointment? I have [Date] at [Time] or [Date] at [Time]. Does either of those work for you? Great, so that's confirmed for [Date] at [Time].
