# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Contact and Intent Gathering

### Greet Caller
**When:** Call initiated
**Action:** Offer a friendly greeting and inquire about the reason for contact.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Clinic Name]. How may I help you today?

### Understand Intent
**When:** After greeting the caller
**Action:** Ask open-ended questions to determine the customer's purpose for calling.
**Example:** What can I assist you with today?

## Patient Identification and Verification

### Inquire Patient Status
**When:** When scheduling or accessing records
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or existing.
**Example:** Are you a new patient with us, or have you visited before?

### Request Date of Birth
**When:** When verifying identity or locating records
**Action:** Ask for the patient's date of birth.
**Example:** To help me find your records, could you please provide your date of birth?

### Request Last Name
**When:** When verifying identity or locating records
**Action:** Ask for the patient's last name.
**Example:** And what is the patient's last name?

## Appointment Management

### Offer Appointment Slots
**When:** Customer expresses intent to schedule an appointment
**Action:** Provide a selection of available dates and times.
**Example:** We have openings on Tuesday at 10 AM or Thursday at 2 PM. Do either of those work for you?

### Confirm Appointment Details
**When:** Customer has selected an appointment time
**Action:** Recapitulate all confirmed appointment details.
**Example:** Okay, so that's an appointment for [Patient Name] on [Date] at [Time] with [Doctor Name]. Is that correct?

## Communication and Clarification

### Acknowledge User Request
**When:** User states a request or provides information
**Action:** Verbally confirm understanding of the user's statement.
**Example:** Okay, I understand you're looking to schedule an appointment.

### Repeat for Clarity
**When:** Customer's response is unclear or incomplete
**Action:** Rephrase or repeat the question to solicit the necessary information.
**Example:** I apologize, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please repeat your date of birth?
