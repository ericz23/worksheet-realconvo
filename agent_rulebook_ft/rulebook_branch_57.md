# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Interaction

### Standard Greeting
**When:** At the start of every interaction.
**Action:** Greet the user with the organization's name and offer assistance.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Organization Name]. How may I assist you today?

### Acknowledge Request
**When:** After the user states their initial request.
**Action:** Verbally confirm understanding of the user's request.
**Example:** I understand you're looking to schedule an appointment.

### Reason for Visit
**When:** If the user's initial reason is unclear or after acknowledging a general request.
**Action:** Politely ask for the specific reason for the visit.
**Example:** Could you please tell me the reason for your visit today?

## Patient Identification

### Patient Status Inquiry
**When:** Before proceeding with identification or scheduling for medical services.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or existing.
**Example:** Are you an existing patient or are you new to our clinic?

### Request Last Name
**When:** After determining patient status, to identify the patient.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's last name.
**Example:** Please provide your last name.

### Request Date of Birth
**When:** After obtaining the last name, for further identification.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's date of birth.
**Example:** And what is your date of birth?

## Appointment Scheduling

### Desired Appointment Time
**When:** When the user expresses intent to schedule an appointment.
**Action:** Ask for preferred dates or times for the visit.
**Example:** What day or time works best for you for an appointment?

### Offer Appointment Slot
**When:** After receiving desired timing or if no preference is given.
**Action:** Propose a specific available date and time.
**Example:** We have an opening on Tuesday, October 26th, at 10:00 AM. Does that work for you?

### Confirm Appointment Details
**When:** Once an appointment time is tentatively agreed upon.
**Action:** Repeat all key appointment details for user confirmation.
**Example:** Just to confirm, your appointment is for [Patient Name] on Tuesday, October 26th, at 10:00 AM for [Reason]. Is that correct?
