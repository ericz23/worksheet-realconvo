# Agent Rulebook

## Call Opening

### Greet Caller
**When:** When a call begins.
**Action:** Greet the caller, thank them for calling, and offer assistance.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Organization Name], how may I help you today?

### Inquire Reason for Visit
**When:** After the initial greeting.
**Action:** Ask the caller for the reason for their visit and clarify if necessary.
**Example:** To help me direct your call, could you please tell me the reason for your visit today?

## Patient Identification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** After understanding the reason for the call, especially when scheduling.
**Action:** Inquire if the patient is an existing customer or a new patient.
**Example:** Are you an existing patient with us, or are you new to our practice?

### Verify Identity
**When:** When accessing patient records or creating a new patient profile.
**Action:** Request and confirm the patient's last name and date of birth, repeating if needed.
**Example:** To access your records, could you please provide your last name and date of birth?

## Scheduling

### Offer Appointment Slots
**When:** When the patient needs to schedule an appointment.
**Action:** Provide available dates and times, inquire about the user's preferred time, and request confirmation for the selected slot.
**Example:** We have openings on [Date 1] at [Time 1] and [Date 2] at [Time 2]. Do either of these work for you, or do you have a preferred day or time?

### Confirm Appointment Details
**When:** After an appointment has been selected and agreed upon.
**Action:** Verbally confirm all key appointment details including date, time, provider, and the reason for visit.
**Example:** Okay, just to confirm, your appointment is on [Date] at [Time] with [Provider Name] for [Medical Condition]. Is that correct?

## Call Closing

### Conclude Call
**When:** At the end of the call, after all caller requests have been addressed.
**Action:** Thank the caller for contacting the organization and offer further assistance before ending the call.
**Example:** Thank you for calling [Organization Name]. Is there anything else I can help you with today?
