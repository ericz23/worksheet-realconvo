# Agent Rulebook

## Greeting and Closing

### Initial Greeting
**When:** When a customer initiates contact
**Action:** Greet the customer, offer assistance, and understand their intent
**Example:** "Hello, thank you for calling. How may I assist you today?"

### Call Closing
**When:** After the customer's primary request has been fulfilled
**Action:** Thank the caller and offer further assistance
**Example:** "Thank you for calling. Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

## Identity Verification and Information Gathering

### Gather Patient Information
**When:** After understanding the customer's intent, when personal details are required for service
**Action:** Collect patient status (existing/new), date of birth, last name, and reason for visit, re-prompting if necessary
**Example:** "To help me find your record, could you please confirm if you are an existing patient or new? Also, what is your date of birth, last name, and the reason for your visit today?"

## Scheduling

### Schedule Appointment
**When:** When the customer expresses a need to book an appointment
**Action:** Determine desired timing, propose specific dates and times, confirm customer availability, and finalize all appointment details
**Example:** "What day and time works best for you? I have an opening on [Date] at [Time] with [Provider]. Does that work for you?"
