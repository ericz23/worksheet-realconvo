# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Contact

### Initiate Interaction
**When:** When starting a new call or interaction.
**Action:** Thank the caller, greet the customer, and offer assistance.
**Example:** Thank you for calling [Clinic Name], my name is [Agent Name], how may I help you today?

## Patient Identification

### Inquire Patient Status
**When:** After the initial greeting, to determine if the patient has an existing record.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or existing.
**Example:** Are you an existing patient with us, or are you new to our clinic today?

### Request Patient Demographics
**When:** After inquiring about patient status, for identity verification.
**Action:** Request the customer's last name and date of birth.
**Example:** Could I please get your last name and date of birth to pull up your records?

## Appointment Management

### Ascertain Visit Reason
**When:** After patient identification, to understand the purpose of the visit.
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the visit.
**Example:** And what is the reason for your visit today?

### Offer Flexible Scheduling
**When:** When arranging a new appointment or follow-up.
**Action:** Offer flexible scheduling options (e.g., 'as soon as possible' or 'specific time frame') and provide specific appointment slots (date and time).
**Example:** Are you looking to come in as soon as possible, or do you have a specific date and time in mind? We have availability on [Date] at [Time] or [Date] at [Time].

### Confirm Appointment Details
**When:** Once an appointment has been scheduled or modified.
**Action:** Confirm all appointment details (date, time, and reason for the visit).
**Example:** Just to confirm, your appointment is set for [Date] at [Time] for [Reason]. Is that correct?

## Customer Interaction

### Express Empathy
**When:** When a customer communicates a problem or negative situation.
**Action:** Express empathy and acknowledge their feelings.
**Example:** I'm sorry to hear that you're going through that. Let's see how I can help you resolve this.

## Call Conclusion

### Conclude Interaction
**When:** At the end of the call, after addressing all customer needs.
**Action:** Thank the caller and offer further assistance.
**Example:** Thank you for calling [Clinic Name], [Patient Name]. Is there anything else I can assist you with today?
