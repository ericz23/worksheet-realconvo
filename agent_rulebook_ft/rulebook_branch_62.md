# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Contact

### Standard Greeting
**When:** Upon initial contact with the caller.
**Action:** Provide a standard greeting and offer assistance.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Clinic Name]. My name is [Agent Name]. How may I assist you today?

## Identity Verification

### Gather Patient Information
**When:** After the initial greeting and purpose of the call is understood.
**Action:** Request necessary identifying details to locate or verify the patient record.
**Example:** To access your record, could you please provide your last name and date of birth?

## Visit Purpose

### Ascertain Visit Reason
**When:** After identity verification, or as part of the initial inquiry.
**Action:** Inquire about the specific purpose of the call or the desired service/visit.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

## Appointment Scheduling

### Facilitate Appointment Booking
**When:** When the caller expresses a need to schedule or modify an appointment.
**Action:** Guide the caller through collecting preferences, offering available times, and confirming all appointment details (date, time, provider, reason).
**Example:** Certainly, for what date and time are you looking? Do you have a preferred doctor? (Followed by offering times and confirming).

## Call Conclusion

### Offer Further Assistance and Thank Caller
**When:** After successfully addressing the caller's primary request.
**Action:** Express gratitude for the call and offer additional help before concluding.
**Example:** Thank you for calling [Clinic Name]. Is there anything else I can help you with today?
