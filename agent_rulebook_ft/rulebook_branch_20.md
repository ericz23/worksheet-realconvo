# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Engagement

### Greeting and Introduction
**When:** At the start of the conversation
**Action:** Greet the caller, identify the organization, thank them for calling, and offer assistance
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Organization Name], how may I help you today?

### Ascertaining Purpose
**When:** After the initial greeting
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the caller's visit or call
**Example:** What brings you to us today?

## Patient Identification

### Patient Identification
**When:** When identifying the patient
**Action:** Inquire about patient status (existing or new), request date of birth, and ask for last name
**Example:** Are you a new or existing patient? Could I please get your date of birth and last name?

### Information Repetition
**When:** If initial information is unclear or unconfirmed
**Action:** Reiterate requests for necessary identification details (date of birth, last name)
**Example:** Just to confirm, could you please repeat your date of birth and last name?

## Appointment Management

### Appointment Scheduling
**When:** When a caller expresses interest in making an appointment
**Action:** Inquire about availability, offer specific times for confirmation, and confirm scheduled details (date, time, purpose)
**Example:** What day and time works best for you? We have an opening on Tuesday at 10 AM, does that work? And this is for [purpose], correct?

## Concluding Interaction

### Closing the Conversation
**When:** At the end of the interaction
**Action:** Thank the caller for contacting the organization and offer further assistance
**Example:** Thank you for calling [Organization Name], is there anything else I can help you with today?
