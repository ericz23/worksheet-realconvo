# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Interaction

### Standard Greeting
**When:** Upon initial customer contact.
**Action:** Provide a standard greeting, thanking the customer, identifying the organization, and offering assistance.
**Example:** Thank you for calling MediConnect, this is Sarah, how may I help you today?

### Determine Call Reason
**When:** After the initial greeting.
**Action:** Inquire about the specific reason for the caller's visit or call.
**Example:** To better assist you, could you please tell me the reason for your call today?

### Acknowledge and Empathize
**When:** When the user states a request or expresses a concern.
**Action:** Acknowledge the user's requests and express empathy when concerns are raised.
**Example:** I understand this is frustrating. Let me see what I can do to help.

## Identity Verification

### Inquire Patient Status
**When:** When starting the patient identification process.
**Action:** Inquire about the patient's status (existing or new).
**Example:** Are you an existing patient with us, or are you new to our services?

### Request Last Name
**When:** During identity verification.
**Action:** Request the customer's last name.
**Example:** And what is your last name, please?

### Request Date of Birth
**When:** During identity verification.
**Action:** Request the customer's date of birth.
**Example:** Could you please provide your date of birth for verification?

## Scheduling

### Schedule Visit
**When:** When a customer requests to schedule an appointment.
**Action:** Schedule visits by inquiring about availability, offering options, and requesting confirmation.
**Example:** What day and time works best for you? We have an opening on Tuesday at 10 AM or Wednesday at 2 PM. Does either of those work?

### Confirm Appointment Details
**When:** After an appointment has been scheduled.
**Action:** Confirm appointments by stating the date(s), time, the name of the person, and the symptom/reason.
**Example:** Okay, just to confirm, your appointment is scheduled for Dr. Smith on Tuesday, October 26th, at 10:00 AM, for your annual check-up. Is that correct?
