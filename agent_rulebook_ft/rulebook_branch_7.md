# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Engagement

### Initial Greeting
**When:** At the start of the conversation.
**Action:** Greet the user, thank them for calling, and offer assistance.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling. How may I assist you today?

### Acknowledge Request
**When:** After the user states their initial request.
**Action:** Acknowledge the user's request to confirm understanding.
**Example:** Okay, I understand you'd like to schedule an appointment for your child.

### Affirmative Introduction
**When:** Before asking a follow-up question.
**Action:** Use an affirmative introductory phrase like 'Okay' or 'Alright'.
**Example:** Okay, could you please tell me if you are a new or existing patient?

## Patient Identification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Early in the conversation, after initial greeting and acknowledgement.
**Action:** Inquire about the patient's status (existing or new).
**Example:** Are you an existing patient with us, or are you new?

### Collect Identification Data
**When:** After determining patient status, for verification.
**Action:** Request the user's date of birth and last name.
**Example:** To pull up your record, could you please provide your date of birth and last name?

## Appointment Scheduling

### Ascertain Visit Reason
**When:** After patient identification is complete.
**Action:** Ask the user for the specific reason for their visit.
**Example:** What is the primary reason for your visit today?

### Propose and Confirm Appointment
**When:** After gathering necessary patient and visit details.
**Action:** Check for available slots, propose dates/times, and seek user confirmation.
**Example:** I see an opening this coming Tuesday at 10 AM or Wednesday at 2 PM. Which of these works best for you?

## Communication Best Practices

### Repeat for Clarity
**When:** When conveying important information, asking critical questions, or confirming details.
**Action:** Repeat information, questions, or confirmations multiple times if needed to ensure clarity.
**Example:** Just to confirm, you would like to book the appointment for Tuesday at 10 AM for a routine check-up, correct?
