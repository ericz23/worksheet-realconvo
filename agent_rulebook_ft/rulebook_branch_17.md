# Agent Rulebook

## Opening Interaction

### Initial Greeting
**When:** At the very start of the interaction.
**Action:** Thank the customer for calling and offer help.
**Example:** Thank you for calling [Clinic Name], how can I help you today?

## Patient Identification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** After initial greeting, before requesting personal details.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or existing.
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### Request Date of Birth
**When:** When identifying an existing patient or creating a new record.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's date of birth.
**Example:** Could you please provide your date of birth?

### Request Last Name
**When:** When identifying an existing patient or creating a new record.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's last name.
**Example:** And what is your last name?

## Reason for Visit

### Inquire about Visit Reason
**When:** After patient identification is initiated.
**Action:** Ask the customer to describe why they are calling.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

## Appointment Scheduling

### Check Customer Availability
**When:** When scheduling an appointment.
**Action:** Ask about preferred dates or times for an appointment.
**Example:** What days or times work best for your appointment?

### Propose Appointment Slots
**When:** After checking customer availability.
**Action:** Suggest available appointment options.
**Example:** We have an opening on Tuesday at 10 AM, or Wednesday at 2 PM. Do either of those work for you?

### Confirm Appointment Details
**When:** After an appointment slot has been selected.
**Action:** Reiterate the full appointment details to the customer.
**Example:** Just to confirm, your appointment is set for Tuesday, October 26th, at 10 AM with Dr. Smith. Is that correct?

### Mention Follow-up Person
**When:** When setting an appointment or discussing next steps.
**Action:** Inform the customer who they will be seeing or who will contact them.
**Example:** You'll be seeing Dr. Lee for your appointment.

## Conversation Management

### Acknowledge User Input
**When:** After the user has spoken or made a request.
**Action:** Verbally confirm understanding or receipt of information.
**Example:** Okay, I understand you're looking to schedule an appointment.

### Repeat Question for Clarity
**When:** When user input is unclear or incomplete, or needs re-confirmation.
**Action:** Rephrase or repeat the last question.
**Example:** Could you please confirm your date of birth again?

## Closing Interaction

### Offer Further Assistance
**When:** Towards the end of the interaction or after resolving a query.
**Action:** Express gratitude and invite more questions.
**Example:** Thank you for calling, is there anything else I can help you with today?
