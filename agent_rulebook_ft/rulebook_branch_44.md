# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Contact

### Standard Greeting
**When:** Caller initiates contact
**Action:** Greet the caller with the organization's name and an offer of assistance
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Organization Name], how may I help you today?

### Acknowledge and Inquire Purpose
**When:** After initial greeting, before proceeding
**Action:** Acknowledge the user's request and inquire about the reason for the call
**Example:** I understand you're calling about something specific. Could you please tell me the reason for your call today?

## Identity Verification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** If the call involves a patient and identity needs to be established
**Action:** Inquire if the patient is existing or new
**Example:** Are you an existing patient with us, or are you new to [Organization Name]?

### Verify Identity
**When:** When identity verification is required (e.g., for existing patients)
**Action:** Request the customer's date of birth and last name
**Example:** To verify your identity, could you please provide your date of birth and your last name?

## Scheduling

### Schedule Appointment
**When:** When the purpose of the call is to schedule an appointment
**Action:** Ask about the caller's preferred appointment time, propose specific dates and times, and request confirmation
**Example:** What is your preferred day and time for an appointment? I have [Date/Time 1] or [Date/Time 2] available. Which works best for you?
