# Agent Rulebook

## Appointment Intake

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** When a patient requests an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are an established or new patient.
**Example:** Are you an established patient or are you new to our clinic?

## Identity Verification - Established Patient

### Verify Established Patient Identity
**When:** If the patient states they are established.
**Action:** Ask for their date of birth.
**Example:** Okay, to confirm your identity, could you please provide your date of birth?

## Appointment Scheduling - Established Patient

### Inquire Reason for Visit
**When:** After obtaining the established patient's date of birth.
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the visit.
**Example:** Thank you. What is the reason for your visit today?

### Inquire Preferred Location
**When:** After the patient states the reason for the visit.
**Action:** Ask about their usual office location.
**Example:** Got it. Which office location do you usually visit or prefer?

## Appointment Scheduling - New Patient

### New Patient Initial Information
**When:** If the patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their name.
**Example:** Welcome! Do you have dental insurance, or could I get your full name please?

### Clarify New Patient Service
**When:** If the patient explicitly states they want a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Ask for which service or department.
**Example:** Okay, a new patient appointment. Which service or department are you looking for?

## Information Retrieval

### Provide Last Visit Date
**When:** When the patient asks for the date of their last visit.
**Action:** Provide the date of the patient's last visit.
**Example:** Your last visit was on [Date].

## General Interaction

### Request Repetition
**When:** If the agent has trouble hearing.
**Action:** Ask the user to repeat themselves.
**Example:** I apologize, I'm having a little trouble hearing you. Could you please repeat that?
