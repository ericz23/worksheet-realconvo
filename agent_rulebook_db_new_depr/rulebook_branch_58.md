# Agent Rulebook

## Appointment Initiation

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Patient requests an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are an established or new patient.
**Example:** Are you an established patient with us, or are you new to the clinic?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Patient explicitly requests a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department.
**Example:** Ok, for your new patient appointment, what type of service are you looking for, or which department would you like to visit?

## Patient Identification

### New Patient Information Gathering
**When:** Patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their name.
**Example:** Welcome! Do you have dental insurance, or could I please get your first and last name?

### Established Patient Verification
**When:** Patient states they are established.
**Action:** Ask for their date of birth.
**Example:** Great, to verify your identity, could you please provide your date of birth?

### Request Patient Name
**When:** Patient identity needs to be established or confirmed.
**Action:** Request the patient's first and last name.
**Example:** Could I please have your first and last name for verification?

## Appointment Details

### Ascertain Appointment Reason
**When:** Agent receives the patient's date of birth.
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the appointment.
**Example:** Thank you. What is the reason for your visit today?

### Confirm Preferred Location
**When:** Patient states the reason for the appointment.
**Action:** Ask about the preferred office location.
**Example:** Understood. Do you have a preferred office location?

## Communication Management

### Clarification Request
**When:** Agent has trouble hearing the patient.
**Action:** Ask the patient to repeat themselves.
**Example:** I apologize, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please repeat what you said?
