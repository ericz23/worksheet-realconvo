# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Identification

### Confirm Patient Status
**When:** A customer expresses a desire to book an appointment.
**Action:** Confirm if they are a new or established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new or established patient with us?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** The patient identifies as new.
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department.
**Example:** Which service or department are you interested in?

### Established Patient ID for Chart
**When:** The patient identifies as established.
**Action:** Ask for identifying information (e.g., phone number, date of birth) to locate their chart.
**Example:** Could you please provide your phone number or date of birth so I can locate your chart?

### Acknowledge Chart Retrieval
**When:** Identifying information for an established patient has been received.
**Action:** Inform the customer that the chart is being located.
**Example:** Thank you, please bear with me while I locate your chart.

## Appointment Details

### Determine Appointment Reason
**When:** The patient's status and relevant initial details (e.g., service for new, chart for established) have been determined.
**Action:** Ask for the type or reason for the appointment.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

### Acknowledge Multiple Reasons
**When:** The customer states multiple reasons for a visit.
**Action:** Acknowledge all stated reasons.
**Example:** I understand you're looking to discuss both X and Y.

## Scheduling

### Inquire Preferred Scheduling Time
**When:** The type or reason for the appointment has been confirmed.
**Action:** Ask about the patient's preferred scheduling time.
**Example:** Do you have any preferred dates or times for your appointment?
