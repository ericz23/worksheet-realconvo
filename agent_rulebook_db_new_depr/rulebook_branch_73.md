# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Patient Classification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Patient initiates contact for an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or established.
**Example:** Are you a new patient, or have you visited us before?

## Identity Verification

### Verify Established Patient Identity
**When:** Patient states they are established.
**Action:** Request their full name and date of birth.
**Example:** To locate your record, could you please provide your full name and date of birth?

### Gather New Patient Initial Information
**When:** Patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or request their full name.
**Example:** Welcome! Do you have dental insurance, or could you provide your full name to get started?

### Retrieve Patient Record
**When:** Agent has received patient's identifying information (name and date of birth).
**Action:** Attempt to look up their record in the system.
**Example:** Thank you, please wait a moment while I access your file.

### Clarify Information
**When:** Patient provides unclear or incomplete information.
**Action:** Request clarification or additional details.
**Example:** Could you please spell that name for me?

## Appointment Logistics

### Determine New Patient Service Needs
**When:** Patient states they want a new patient appointment.
**Action:** Ask for the specific service or department they need.
**Example:** Certainly, what type of service or department are you looking to schedule your first visit with?

### Confirm Preferred Office Location
**When:** Patient identity and reason for visit are confirmed.
**Action:** Inquire about their preferred or usual office location.
**Example:** Before we look at availability, which of our office locations would be most convenient for your visit?
