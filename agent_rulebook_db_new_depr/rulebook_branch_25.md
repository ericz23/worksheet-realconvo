# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Identification and Chart Retrieval

### Patient Status Inquiry
**When:** Initial patient contact.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or established.
**Example:** Are you a new patient or an established patient with us?

### Established Patient Identification
**When:** Patient states they are established.
**Action:** Ask for identifying information (e.g., phone number, date of birth, last name) to locate their chart.
**Example:** To locate your chart, could you please provide your phone number, date of birth, or last name?

### Chart Retrieval Acknowledgment
**When:** Identifying information received for an established patient.
**Action:** Acknowledge receipt of information and state chart retrieval is in progress.
**Example:** Thank you. Please give me a moment while I pull up your chart.

### Confirm Visit Reason (Established Patient)
**When:** Chart retrieved for an established patient.
**Action:** Clarify the reason for the appointment.
**Example:** Okay, I have your chart here. What can I help you with today?

## Service Determination and Scheduling

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department.
**Example:** What type of service are you looking for, or which department are you trying to reach?

### Appointment Availability Check
**When:** Patient identity is confirmed and the purpose of the appointment is clarified.
**Action:** Ask about the patient's availability to schedule the appointment.
**Example:** Alright, now that I understand what you need, when would be a good time for you to come in for an appointment?
