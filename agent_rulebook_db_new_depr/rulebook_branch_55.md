# Agent Rulebook

## Appointment Booking & Status Check

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** When a patient requests an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are an established or new patient.
**Example:** Are you an established patient or new to our practice?

### Clarify New Patient Service
**When:** If a patient explicitly requests a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Ask for the service or department.
**Example:** Which service or department are you looking for?

## Established Patient Workflow

### Verify Established Patient Identity
**When:** If the patient states they are established.
**Action:** Ask for their date of birth.
**Example:** Could you please confirm your date of birth?

### Gather Appointment Reason (Established)
**When:** After confirming the date of birth.
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the appointment.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

### Identify Preferred Location (Established)
**When:** After the patient states the reason for the visit.
**Action:** Ask for their usual office location.
**Example:** Which of our office locations do you usually visit?

### Check Last Physical Date
**When:** When a patient requests an annual physical.
**Action:** Inquire about the date of their last physical.
**Example:** When was your last annual physical?

## New Patient Onboarding

### Gather New Patient Information
**When:** If the patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their name.
**Example:** Do you have dental insurance, or could I get your full name please?

## Patient & Provider Information

### Provide Last Visit Date
**When:** If requested by the patient.
**Action:** Provide the patient's last visit date.
**Example:** Your last visit was on [Date].

### Clarify Doctor Details
**When:** If asked by the patient about a specific doctor.
**Action:** Clarify details about that doctor.
**Example:** Certainly, what would you like to know about Dr. [Doctor's Name]?

## General Communication

### Request Repetition for Clarity
**When:** If the agent has trouble hearing the patient.
**Action:** Ask the user to repeat themselves.
**Example:** I apologize, I'm having a little trouble hearing you. Could you please repeat that?
