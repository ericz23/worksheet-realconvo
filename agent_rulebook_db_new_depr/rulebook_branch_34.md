# Agent Rulebook

## Scheduling

### Initial Appointment Inquiry
**When:** Customer expresses a desire to book an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are a new or established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new or established patient?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Customer explicitly states they want a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Ask for which service or department.
**Example:** Which service or department are you looking to schedule for?

### Post-Insurance Scheduling Offer
**When:** Insurance details are clarified.
**Action:** Offer to check availability and explain new patient initial visit requirements.
**Example:** With your insurance details clarified, I can now check availability and explain the requirements for your new patient initial visit.

## Patient Onboarding

### Gather New Patient Name
**When:** New patient confirmed.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's name.
**Example:** What is your full name?

### Last Medical Checkup
**When:** New patient confirmed.
**Action:** Ask about the patient's last medical checkup.
**Example:** When was your last medical checkup?

## Insurance Verification

### Initial Dental Insurance Check
**When:** New patient confirmed.
**Action:** Ask if they have dental insurance.
**Example:** Do you have dental insurance?

### Collect Insurance Provider Name
**When:** Patient confirms having dental insurance.
**Action:** Ask for the name of their insurance.
**Example:** What is the name of your insurance provider?

### Collect Insurance Plan Type
**When:** Patient provides the insurance name.
**Action:** Ask for the insurance plan type (e.g., PPO, HMO, state insurance).
**Example:** And what type of plan do you have? For example, PPO, HMO, or state insurance?

### Out-of-Network Disclosure
**When:** Office is out of network for their insurance.
**Action:** Inform the customer.
**Example:** Please be aware that our office is out of network with your current insurance plan.

### Clarify Insurance Name Request
**When:** Customer asks for clarification about the insurance name.
**Action:** Repeat the question about the insurance name.
**Example:** I was asking for the name of your insurance provider.

## General Conversation Management

### Acknowledge Information Check
**When:** Customer asks for a moment to check information.
**Action:** Acknowledge the request.
**Example:** Okay, take your time.
