# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Contact & Patient Identification

### Identify Patient Type
**When:** Patient requests an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are an established or new patient.
**Example:** Are you an established patient or new to our practice?

### Verify Established Patient Identity
**When:** Patient states they are established.
**Action:** Request their date of birth, then first and last name for identification.
**Example:** To verify your identity, could you please provide your date of birth, followed by your first and last name?

### New Patient Initial Information
**When:** Patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their first and last name.
**Example:** Welcome! Do you have dental insurance, or could I get your first and last name to start?

## Established Patient Appointment Scheduling

### Lookup Established Patient Records
**When:** Existing patient identified.
**Action:** Initiate a lookup of their records.
**Example:** Thank you, I'm pulling up your records now.

### Inquire Appointment Reason
**When:** Existing patient identified.
**Action:** Inquire about the reason for their appointment.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

### Ask Usual Office Location
**When:** Established patient states the reason for their visit.
**Action:** Ask about their usual office location.
**Example:** And which of our office locations do you usually visit?

## New Patient Appointment Scheduling

### Inquire Service or Department
**When:** A new patient appointment is explicitly requested.
**Action:** Ask for the service or department needed.
**Example:** What type of service are you looking for, or which department do you need to see?

## General Communication Protocols

### Acknowledge Information Receipt
**When:** The patient provides information.
**Action:** Acknowledge receipt of the information.
**Example:** Got it, thank you for that.

### Request Repetition
**When:** The agent has trouble hearing.
**Action:** Ask the user to repeat themselves.
**Example:** I apologize, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please repeat what you said?
