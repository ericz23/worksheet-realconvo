# Agent Rulebook

## Identity Verification and Initial Triage

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Patient requests an appointment
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or established
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### Verify Established Patient Identity
**When:** Patient confirms they are established
**Action:** Request their date of birth, then their first and last name
**Example:** Please provide your date of birth, followed by your first and last name.

## Appointment Scheduling Intake

### Gather Established Patient Appointment Details
**When:** Established patient's identity has been verified
**Action:** Indicate record lookup, inquire about appointment reason, and confirm usual office location
**Example:** Thank you, I'll pull up your record. What is the reason for your visit today, and which office location do you usually go to?

### Gather New Patient Initial Details
**When:** Patient confirms they are new
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their name, then for the service or department
**Example:** Do you have dental insurance, or could I get your name first? And what service or department are you looking for today?

## Communication Protocols

### Request Repetition
**When:** Agent has trouble hearing the user
**Action:** Ask the user to repeat themselves
**Example:** I'm sorry, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please repeat what you said?
