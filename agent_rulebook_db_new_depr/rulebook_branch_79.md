# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Appointment Intake

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Patient requests an appointment
**Action:** Ask if patient is new or established
**Example:** Are you a new or established patient?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Patient explicitly states they want a 'new patient appointment'
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department
**Example:** What service are you looking for, or which department would you like to visit?

## Patient Identification and Verification

### Basic Identification
**When:** Identification is required
**Action:** Request the patient's name and date of birth
**Example:** Could I get your full name and date of birth, please?

### New Patient Information
**When:** Patient is new
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their name
**Example:** Do you have dental insurance, or could I please get your full name?

### Established Patient Verification
**When:** Patient is established
**Action:** Request their date of birth for verification
**Example:** Could you please confirm your date of birth?

### Clarify Ambiguous Name
**When:** Patient name is ambiguous or unclear
**Action:** Clarify the patient's name
**Example:** Could you spell that for me, please?

### Patient Record Lookup
**When:** After receiving identifying information
**Action:** Pause to look up the patient's record
**Example:** Please give me a moment while I pull up your file.

## Appointment Details and Scheduling

### Determine Reason for Visit
**When:** After identifying the patient
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the visit and specific type of appointment
**Example:** What's the reason for your visit today, and is it a regular check-up or something more urgent?

### Preferred Location/Clinic Inquiry
**When:** After determining the reason for visit
**Action:** Ask about preferred locations or usual clinics
**Example:** Do you have a preferred location or clinic you usually visit?

## Patient Information Access

### Provide Last Visit Details
**When:** Patient requests details about their last visit
**Action:** Provide details about the patient's last visit
**Example:** Your last visit was on [Date] for a [Reason].

## Communication Protocol

### Clarify Audio Issues
**When:** Agent has trouble hearing the patient
**Action:** Ask the patient to repeat themselves
**Example:** Could you please repeat that? I'm having a little trouble hearing you.
