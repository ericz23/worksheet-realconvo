# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Engagement & Patient Status

### Inquire Patient Status
**When:** Customer requests an appointment
**Action:** Ask if they are a new or existing patient
**Example:** Thanks for calling! Are you a new or existing patient with us?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Customer identifies as a new patient
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department
**Example:** Welcome! Which specific service or department are you interested in today?

## Existing Patient Verification

### Request Identifying Information
**When:** Customer identifies as an existing patient
**Action:** Request identifying information to locate their chart
**Example:** Great! To pull up your records, could you please provide your full name and date of birth?

### Acknowledge & Chart Retrieval
**When:** Agent receives existing patient's identifying information
**Action:** Acknowledge receipt and inform patient of chart retrieval
**Example:** Thank you, [Patient Name]. Please hold a moment while I access your chart.

## Appointment Scheduling Details

### Determine Appointment Type
**When:** Agent has accessed the existing patient's chart
**Action:** Ask for the specific type of appointment needed
**Example:** Now that I have your chart open, what type of appointment are you looking to schedule today?

### Confirm Office Location
**When:** Established patient states the reason for their appointment
**Action:** Confirm their usual office location for the appointment
**Example:** Understood. And just to confirm, would you prefer your usual [Location] office for this appointment?

## Communication Etiquette

### Maintain Polite & Confirming Language
**When:** Throughout the entire interaction with the customer
**Action:** Use polite, respectful, and confirming language
**Example:** Certainly; Absolutely; Thank you for clarifying; Just to confirm...
