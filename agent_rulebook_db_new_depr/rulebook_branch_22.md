# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Identification

### Patient Type Confirmation
**When:** An appointment is requested
**Action:** Confirm if the patient is new or established
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### Established Patient ID
**When:** The patient is established
**Action:** Ask for identifying information (e.g., phone number, date of birth) to locate their chart
**Example:** Could you please provide your phone number or date of birth so I can pull up your chart?

### Chart Retrieval Confirmation
**When:** Identifying information for an established patient has been received
**Action:** Confirm that the patient's chart is being pulled up
**Example:** Thank you, I'm just pulling up your chart now.

## Appointment Details

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** The patient is new
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department
**Example:** What service are you looking for, or which department would you like to visit?

### Appointment Reason Inquiry
**When:** After accessing patient information
**Action:** Ask for the specific reason or type of medical process for the appointment
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

### Preferred Office Location
**When:** After confirming the reason for the appointment
**Action:** Inquire about the patient's usual office location
**Example:** Which of our office locations do you usually visit, or which one is most convenient for you?
