# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Patient Contact & Identification

### Identify Patient Type
**When:** Customer requests an appointment
**Action:** Ask if they are an established or new patient
**Example:** Are you an established patient with us, or are you a new patient?

### New Patient Information
**When:** Patient states they are new
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance, their name, or desired service/department
**Example:** Great, could you tell me your full name, and do you have dental insurance?

### Established Patient Verification
**When:** Patient states they are established
**Action:** Request their date of birth, then their first and last name
**Example:** Okay, to pull up your records, could you please provide your date of birth, and then your first and last name?

### Look Up Patient Records
**When:** Identifying information collected for an established patient
**Action:** Initiate a lookup of their records
**Example:** (Agent types, searching for records)

## Appointment Details

### Inquire Appointment Reason
**When:** Patient successfully identified
**Action:** Inquire about the reason for their appointment
**Example:** Now that I've found your account, what can we help you with today?

## General Communication Protocols

### Acknowledge Information
**When:** Information received from the patient
**Action:** Acknowledge receipt of information
**Example:** Okay, thank you for that.

### Request Repetition
**When:** Agent has trouble hearing
**Action:** Ask the patient to repeat themselves
**Example:** I apologize, could you please repeat that?
