# Agent Rulebook

## Appointment Booking & Patient Identification

### Initial Appointment Inquiry
**When:** Customer expresses a desire to book an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are an established or new patient.
**Example:** Are you an established patient with us, or are you looking to become a new patient?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Customer explicitly states they want a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Ask for which service or department.
**Example:** Certainly, for what service or department are you looking to schedule your new patient appointment?

### Established Patient Identification
**When:** Patient is identified as established.
**Action:** Ask for their phone number to pull up their chart.
**Example:** Okay, since you're an established patient, could I please get the best phone number associated with your account so I can pull up your chart?

## Chart Access & Appointment Confirmation

### Chart Retrieval Acknowledgment
**When:** Established patient provides their phone number.
**Action:** Acknowledge the information and inform them you will take a moment to pull up the chart.
**Example:** Thank you for that number. Please allow me a moment to pull up your chart.

### Confirm Reason and Check Availability
**When:** Agent has pulled up the patient's chart.
**Action:** Confirm the reason for the appointment and then check for availability.
**Example:** I see your chart here. What is the reason for your visit today? Once I have that, I can check our availability for you.

## General Interaction

### Acknowledge Corrected Information
**When:** Patient provides corrected information.
**Action:** Acknowledge the correction.
**Example:** Thank you for clarifying that.
