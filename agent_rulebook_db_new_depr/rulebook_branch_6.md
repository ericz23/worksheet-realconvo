# Agent Rulebook

## Appointment Initiation

### Identify Patient Type
**When:** Customer expresses desire to book an appointment
**Action:** Confirm if new or established patient
**Example:** Are you a new patient with us, or have you visited before?

## New Patient Onboarding

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** New patient appointment requested
**Action:** Ask for desired service or department
**Example:** What type of service are you looking for, or which department would you like to see?

## Established Patient Identity Verification

### Request Established Patient ID
**When:** Patient identifies as established
**Action:** Request phone number to confirm identity via chart lookup
**Example:** Could I please get the phone number associated with your account to pull up your details?

### Acknowledge ID & Chart Lookup
**When:** After receiving identifying information
**Action:** Acknowledge receipt and state chart lookup process
**Example:** Thank you. Please give me a moment while I pull up your chart.

## Scheduling Details

### Inquire Appointment Reason (Established)
**When:** Established patient's identity confirmed and chart pulled
**Action:** Ask about the reason for the appointment
**Example:** Now that I have your chart open, what is the reason for your visit today?

### Inquire Appointment Availability (Established)
**When:** Reason for established patient's visit confirmed
**Action:** Ask about preferred appointment time or availability
**Example:** Okay, I see. What days or times work best for your appointment?
