# Agent Rulebook

## Appointment Initial Screening

### Confirm Patient Status
**When:** Customer requests an appointment.
**Action:** Agent confirms if they are a new or established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Customer requests a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Agent asks for the desired service or department.
**Example:** What type of service are you looking for, or which department would you like to visit?

## Established Patient Identification

### Request Established Patient ID
**When:** Patient confirms they are established.
**Action:** Agent requests identifying information (e.g., phone number, date of birth, name) to locate their chart.
**Example:** To pull up your chart, could you please provide your full name, date of birth, or phone number?

### Confirm Chart Access
**When:** Agent receives identifying information for an established patient.
**Action:** Agent confirms they are pulling up the patient's chart.
**Example:** Thank you, please give me a moment while I pull up your patient chart.

## Appointment Details Gathering

### Ascertain Appointment Reason
**When:** Agent has accessed patient information.
**Action:** Agent asks for the specific reason or type of medical process for the appointment.
**Example:** Now that I have your chart, what is the reason for your visit today?

### Inquire About Provider Preference
**When:** Agent has accessed an established patient's chart.
**Action:** Agent asks about provider preference.
**Example:** Do you have a specific doctor or provider you'd like to see?

### Inquire About Location Preference
**When:** No provider preference is indicated by the patient.
**Action:** Agent may ask about location preference.
**Example:** Okay, if you don't have a specific provider in mind, do you have a preferred clinic location?
