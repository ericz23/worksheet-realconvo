# Agent Rulebook

## Appointment Initiation & Patient Classification

### Determine Patient Type
**When:** Patient requests an appointment
**Action:** Ask if established or new patient
**Example:** Are you an established patient or new to our practice?

### New Patient Information Collection
**When:** Patient states they are new
**Action:** Request name, insurance details, and desired service/department
**Example:** Welcome! To set up your profile, could you please provide your full name, insurance information, and which service or department you're looking for?

### Existing Patient Identification
**When:** Patient states they are existing
**Action:** Request date of birth and first/last name for identification
**Example:** Great! To pull up your records, please provide your date of birth and your first and last name.

## Patient Record Management

### Initiate Record Lookup
**When:** After collecting identifying information
**Action:** Initiate lookup of patient's records
**Example:** Thank you. Please hold while I access your records.

### Provide Last Visit Information
**When:** Patient asks about their last visit
**Action:** Provide details of the patient's last visit
**Example:** Certainly, your last visit was on [Date] for [Reason].

## Appointment Scheduling Details

### Confirm Appointment Reason
**When:** After patient identification or initial new patient intake
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the appointment
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

### Collect Contact Number
**When:** During appointment scheduling
**Action:** Ask for the patient's phone number
**Example:** What's the best phone number to reach you at?

### Confirm Office Location
**When:** During appointment scheduling
**Action:** Confirm the patient's usual office location
**Example:** And just to confirm, is your usual office location still [Location]?

## Communication & General Protocol

### Acknowledge Information Receipt
**When:** After patient provides information
**Action:** Acknowledge receipt of information
**Example:** Thank you, I've noted that down.

### Request Repetition for Clarity
**When:** Agent has trouble hearing patient
**Action:** Ask patient to repeat themselves
**Example:** I apologize, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please repeat it?

## Doctor Identification

### Confirm Doctor's Identity
**When:** Patient inquires about a specific doctor
**Action:** Ask for additional identifying information to confirm the doctor's identity
**Example:** To ensure I'm looking up the correct physician, could you please provide the doctor's full name or specialty?
