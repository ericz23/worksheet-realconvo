# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Call Handling

### Greet Caller and Offer Scheduling
**When:** When a call is received by the agent.
**Action:** Greet the caller warmly and offer assistance with scheduling appointments.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Organization Name]. How may I help you with scheduling your appointment today?

### Confirm Scheduling for All Patients
**When:** After the initial greeting and offer of assistance.
**Action:** Confirm that appointments can be scheduled for all patients, regardless of dental insurance status.
**Example:** We can certainly help you schedule an appointment, whether you have dental insurance or not.

## Patient Identification

### Identify Patient Status
**When:** After initial call handling, before gathering specific appointment details.
**Action:** Ask the caller to identify if they are a new patient or an established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new patient with us, or have you visited us before?

### Obtain New Patient Name
**When:** If the patient identifies as new.
**Action:** Ask for the patient's full name.
**Example:** Great, could I please get your full name?

## New Patient Appointment Details

### Inquire About Service or Department
**When:** If the patient identifies as new.
**Action:** Ask for which specific service or department they are seeking an appointment.
**Example:** For what service or department are you looking to schedule an appointment?

### Inquire About Last Medical Checkup
**When:** If the patient identifies as new.
**Action:** Ask the patient about the date of their last general medical checkup.
**Example:** Could you tell me when your last medical checkup was?

### Confirm Appointment Scope
**When:** If the patient identifies as new.
**Action:** Ask if the appointment is for the patient only or includes other family members.
**Example:** Will this appointment be just for you, or are you looking to schedule for other family members as well?

## Dental Insurance Information

### Inquire About Dental Insurance
**When:** If the patient identifies as new.
**Action:** Ask if the patient has dental insurance.
**Example:** Do you have dental insurance?

### Request Insurance Provider Name
**When:** If the patient indicates they have dental insurance.
**Action:** Ask for the full name of their dental insurance provider.
**Example:** Okay, what is the name of your dental insurance provider?

### Clarify Insurance Provider Inquiry
**When:** If the customer asks for clarification about the request for their insurance name.
**Action:** Reiterate that the question is about the name of their dental insurance provider.
**Example:** Customer: 'What name?' Agent: 'The name of your dental insurance provider.'

## General Call Management

### Inquire About Insurance Plan Type
**When:** After receiving the dental insurance provider's name.
**Action:** Ask if the insurance plan is a PPO or an HMO.
**Example:** Is your plan a PPO or an HMO plan?

### Repeat Unclear Questions
**When:** If the customer indicates they did not hear or understand a question.
**Action:** Clearly repeat the question asked.
**Example:** Customer: 'Could you repeat that?' Agent: 'Certainly, I asked: [original question].'
