# Agent Rulebook

## Appointment Initiation and Patient Type Identification

### Inquire Patient Status
**When:** A patient requests an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are a new or established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new or established patient?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** The patient explicitly states they want a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department.
**Example:** What type of service are you looking for, or which department?

## Patient Identification and Verification

### Request Established Patient DOB
**When:** The patient states they are established.
**Action:** Request their date of birth.
**Example:** Can I get your date of birth, please?

### Request New Patient Information
**When:** The patient states they are new.
**Action:** Request their full name or ask about dental insurance.
**Example:** Could I please get your full name, or do you have dental insurance?

### Clarify Ambiguous Name
**When:** The patient's name is ambiguous or unclear.
**Action:** Ask the patient to spell or clarify their name.
**Example:** Could you please spell that for me?

### Look Up Patient Record
**When:** After receiving identifying information (e.g., name, date of birth).
**Action:** Look up the patient's record in the system.
**Example:** Please hold while I look up your information.

## Appointment Details Inquiry

### Inquire Reason for Visit
**When:** After confirming patient identity.
**Action:** Ask the reason for the visit.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

## Communication Handling

### Request Repetition Due to Hearing Difficulty
**When:** The agent has trouble hearing the patient.
**Action:** Ask the patient to repeat themselves.
**Example:** I apologize, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please repeat yourself?
