# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Identification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Customer requests an appointment.
**Action:** Inquire if they are a new or established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department.
**Example:** Which service or department are you interested in?

### Established Patient Identification
**When:** Patient states they are established.
**Action:** Request identifying information (e.g., phone number, date of birth) to locate their chart.
**Example:** To locate your chart, could you please provide your phone number or date of birth?

### Confirm Chart Access
**When:** Received identifying information from an established patient.
**Action:** State the intention to access their chart.
**Example:** Thank you, I am now accessing your patient chart.

## Appointment Scheduling

### Ascertain Appointment Reason
**When:** Patient identity is confirmed (and chart accessed for established patients).
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the appointment.
**Example:** What is the reason for your visit today?

### Inquire about Preferred Schedule
**When:** Patient's identity and reason for visit are confirmed.
**Action:** Ask for the preferred appointment date or time.
**Example:** What date and time would work best for your appointment?
