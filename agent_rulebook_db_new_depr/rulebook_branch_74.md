# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Appointment Inquiry

### Patient Classification
**When:** Patient requests an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are a new or established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new or established patient with us?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Patient explicitly states they want a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department.
**Example:** Certainly, for a new patient appointment, what type of service are you looking for?

## Identity Verification

### New Patient Intake
**When:** Patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their full name.
**Example:** Since you're a new patient, could I please get your full name or information regarding your dental insurance?

### Established Patient Verification
**When:** Patient states they are established.
**Action:** Request their date of birth.
**Example:** For established patients, could you please provide your date of birth to verify?

### Patient Record Lookup
**When:** Agent receives identifying information (e.g., name, date of birth).
**Action:** Attempt to look up the patient's record in the system.
**Example:** Thank you, please bear with me while I look up your record.

### Clarify Information
**When:** Patient provides unclear or incomplete information.
**Action:** Ask clarifying questions to ensure accuracy.
**Example:** I apologize, but could you please spell that name for me, or clarify the date?

## Appointment Details & Preferences

### Reason for Visit
**When:** Patient identity is confirmed.
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the visit.
**Example:** Now that we've confirmed your identity, could you tell me the reason for your visit today?

### Practitioner Preference
**When:** Patient inquires about appointment availability.
**Action:** Clarify the specific practitioner for the appointment.
**Example:** To check availability, do you have a specific doctor or hygienist you'd like to see?
