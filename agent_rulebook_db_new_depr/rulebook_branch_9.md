# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Identification & Verification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Customer desires to book an appointment
**Action:** Agent asks if they are a new or established patient
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Customer is a new patient
**Action:** Agent asks for the desired service or department
**Example:** Great! What type of service are you looking for, or which department would you like to visit?

### Established Patient ID Request
**When:** Customer is an established patient
**Action:** Agent requests identifying information (e.g., phone number, date of birth, name) to access their records
**Example:** Welcome back! To pull up your records, could you please provide your phone number and date of birth?

### Acknowledge ID & Retrieve Chart
**When:** Agent has received identifying information from an established patient
**Action:** Agent acknowledges receipt and states intention to retrieve the patient's chart
**Example:** Thank you. Let me quickly pull up your chart.

## Appointment Details & Scheduling

### Inquire Appointment Reason
**When:** Patient is identified and their chart retrieved (or new patient service determined)
**Action:** Agent asks for the specific reason for the appointment
**Example:** Okay, what is the specific reason for your visit today?

### Confirm Preferred Location/Doctor
**When:** Reason for the visit is provided
**Action:** Agent confirms the patient's usual office location or doctor
**Example:** Got it. And do you usually see a specific doctor or prefer a particular office location?

### Request Appointment Timeframe
**When:** Preferred location/doctor is confirmed
**Action:** Agent asks for the patient's desired appointment timeframe
**Example:** What days or times work best for you for an appointment?
