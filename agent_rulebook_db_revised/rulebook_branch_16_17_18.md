# Agent Rulebook

## General Interaction

### Acknowledge Patient Response
**When:** Any patient response is received.
**Action:** Acknowledge the patient's response verbally.
**Example:** Okay, thank you for that information.

## Patient Status

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** During initial patient contact or when booking an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or an established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

## Insurance Management

### Inquire About Dental Insurance (New Patient)
**When:** Patient confirms they are new.
**Action:** Ask if the patient has dental insurance.
**Example:** As a new patient, do you have dental insurance?

### Determine Insurance Plan Type
**When:** Patient confirms having dental insurance.
**Action:** Ask for the insurance plan type (PPO/HMO).
**Example:** Is your dental plan a PPO or an HMO?

### Offer Assistance for Insurance Details
**When:** Patient expresses uncertainty about insurance details (plan type, office assignment, or finding information).
**Action:** Offer to help identify the necessary information.
**Example:** No worries at all, I can help you figure out those details.

### Suggest Insurance Information Resources
**When:** Patient is unsure about insurance details or how to find them.
**Action:** Suggest checking the insurance card or provider's website.
**Example:** You can often find that information on your insurance card or by logging into your provider's website.

### Guide Insurance Card Plan Type Location
**When:** Patient struggles to find plan type on their insurance card.
**Action:** Instruct on specific locations/keywords to look for on the insurance card.
**Example:** On your insurance card, look for 'PPO' or 'HMO' near the plan name, often on the front or back.

### Clarify Provider Network/Office Assignment
**When:** Patient's insurance plan network or office assignment needs clarification.
**Action:** Ask if they are assigned to a specific office or can choose any provider.
**Example:** Does your insurance plan allow you to see any dental provider, or are you restricted to a specific network or office?

### Request Insurance Member ID
**When:** Insurance details are required for lookup or verification after patient confirms having insurance.
**Action:** Ask the patient for their member ID or policy number.
**Example:** Could you please provide your member ID or policy number from your insurance card?

### Acknowledge Insurance ID and Initiate Lookup
**When:** Patient has provided their insurance member ID or policy number.
**Action:** Confirm receipt of the ID and inform the patient that verification is in progress.
**Example:** Thank you, I have your policy number. Please bear with me while I look up your insurance details.

### Perform Insurance Verification
**When:** Insurance details (member ID, plan type, assignment) are ready for verification.
**Action:** Access the system to verify patient's insurance information.
**Example:** Understood. I will now proceed to verify your insurance details on our system.
