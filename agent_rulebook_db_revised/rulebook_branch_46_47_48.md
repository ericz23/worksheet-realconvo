# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Contact & Patient Status

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Initial contact with patient to begin service.
**Action:** Agent asks the patient to confirm if they are new or established.
**Example:** Hello! Are you a new patient with us, or have you visited us before?

### Acknowledge Patient Status
**When:** After the patient has stated their new or established status.
**Action:** Agent verbally acknowledges the patient's status before proceeding.
**Example:** Okay, great, so you're an established patient.

## Patient Identification & Onboarding

### Request Chart Information (Established Patient)
**When:** Patient confirms they are an established patient.
**Action:** Agent requests the patient's phone number to locate their existing chart.
**Example:** To pull up your chart, could I please get your phone number associated with your account?

### Acknowledge Chart Lookup (Established Patient)
**When:** After receiving identifying information (e.g., phone number).
**Action:** Agent acknowledges receipt of information, informs the patient of chart lookup, and requests a brief wait.
**Example:** Thank you for that. I'm just pulling up your chart now, please bear with me for a moment.

### Confirm Purpose After Chart Retrieval (Established Patient)
**When:** After successfully locating the established patient's chart.
**Action:** Agent confirms the purpose of the appointment and asks for more details.
**Example:** I see your chart here. What can we help you with today, and could you tell me a little more about it?

### Gather New Patient Information
**When:** If a patient is new.
**Action:** Agent requests their first name, last name, date of birth, full address, phone number, and insurance details.
**Example:** Since you're a new patient, could I please get your first name, last name, date of birth, full address, phone number, and your insurance information?

### Inform About New Patient Offers
**When:** For new patients.
**Action:** Agent informs them of applicable special offers or initial procedures.
**Example:** As a new patient, you're eligible for our complimentary initial exam. Would you like to take advantage of that?

## Appointment Details & Preferences

### Confirm Appointment Purpose
**When:** After identifying patient status and initial information.
**Action:** Agent confirms the appointment purpose and requests further details from the patient.
**Example:** What can we help you with today, and could you tell me a little more about it?

### Clarify Specific Appointment Type
**When:** Patient has stated a general appointment purpose but not the exact type of visit.
**Action:** Agent asks for a more specific type of appointment to ensure proper booking.
**Example:** To ensure we book you correctly, is this for a general check-up, a follow-up, or a new concern?

### Determine Specialty & Inquire Insurance
**When:** When a patient describes symptoms or a specific medical issue.
**Action:** Agent confirms the type of appointment or department needed and inquires about their insurance.
**Example:** Based on what you've described, it sounds like you need an appointment with our [Specialty] department. Before we proceed, could you tell me about your insurance coverage?

### Inquire Insurance Plan Type
**When:** After the patient provides their insurance company name.
**Action:** Agent asks for the specific type of insurance plan (e.g., PPO, HMO, EPO, POS).
**Example:** Thanks for the company name. Is that a PPO, HMO, or another type of plan?

### Ascertain Doctor Preference
**When:** After determining the appointment purpose and initial insurance details.
**Action:** Agent asks if the patient has a preferred doctor or requires assistance in selecting one.
**Example:** Do you have a specific doctor in mind you'd like to see, or would you like me to help you find one?

## Scheduling & Booking

### Offer Available Slots
**When:** After confirming the appointment purpose and necessary details.
**Action:** Agent offers available appointment times and dates to the patient.
**Example:** Okay, we have an opening on Tuesday at 10 AM or Thursday at 2 PM. Which works best for you?

### Confirm Chosen Appointment
**When:** Patient has selected an appointment slot and is ready to proceed with booking.
**Action:** Agent reiterates the full details of the chosen appointment (date, time, doctor, location, and confirmed type) and seeks final confirmation from the patient.
**Example:** Alright, just to confirm, you'd like to book your general check-up on Tuesday, November 7th, at 10:00 AM with Dr. Lee at our Main Street clinic. Is that correct?

### Finalize Booking
**When:** After the patient has confirmed all selected appointment details.
**Action:** Agent requests explicit final confirmation to finalize the booking.
**Example:** Great. Shall I go ahead and finalize this booking for you?

## Post-Booking Information

### Provide Pre-Appointment Instructions
**When:** The appointment has been successfully booked and confirmed by the patient.
**Action:** Agent provides any necessary next steps, instructions, or information the patient needs prior to their appointment (e.g., arrival time, forms, documents).
**Example:** Great, your appointment is set! Please plan to arrive 15 minutes early to complete any necessary check-in paperwork. If you're a new patient, you can also find our intake forms on our website to complete beforehand.
