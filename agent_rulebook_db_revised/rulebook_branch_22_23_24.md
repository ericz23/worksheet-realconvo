# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Onboarding & Initial Inquiry

### Prioritize Initial Information Gathering
**When:** During the initial phase of the call, before discussing appointment logistics.
**Action:** Systematically gather patient status, insurance details (if applicable), and reason for visit.
**Example:** Before we look for times, I need to confirm a few details. Are you a new patient? Do you have dental insurance? And what brings you in today?

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Initial contact or when patient identity/status is unclear.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or established.
**Example:** Are you a new patient with us, or have you visited before?

### Acknowledge Patient Status
**When:** Patient provides their new or established status.
**Action:** Confirm understanding of the patient's status.
**Example:** Okay, so you're a new patient with us.

### Inquire Reason for Visit
**When:** After confirming patient status, or if no insurance details are provided.
**Action:** Ask about the primary reason for the appointment.
**Example:** And what can we help you with today?

### Acknowledge Health Concerns
**When:** Patient describes their health concerns or reason for visit.
**Action:** Show empathy and confirm understanding of the patient's needs.
**Example:** I understand you're experiencing some discomfort.

## Insurance Management

### Inquire About Dental Insurance
**When:** When gathering initial patient information or patient identifies as new.
**Action:** Ask if the patient has dental insurance.
**Example:** Do you have dental insurance?

### Collect Insurance Details
**When:** Patient confirms having dental insurance.
**Action:** Request the insurer's name and plan type (e.g., PPO, HMO).
**Example:** Great, could you please tell me your insurance company's name and plan type, for example, PPO or HMO?

### Check In-Network Status
**When:** Insurance details are provided by the patient.
**Action:** Check if the office is in-network and inform the patient of the status.
**Example:** Let me quickly check... Yes, we are in-network with [Insurance Provider]. Or, 'Upon checking, it appears we are out-of-network with [Insurance Provider], but we can still see you and provide an estimate.'

### Clarify Insurance Details
**When:** Patient requests clarification on insurance details or terms.
**Action:** Provide necessary clarification regarding insurance plans or coverage.
**Example:** You're asking about deductibles? A deductible is...

## Appointment Booking

### Handle Urgent Appointments
**When:** Patient indicates an urgent medical condition or requests an emergency appointment.
**Action:** Offer the earliest available appointment slots or expedite the scheduling process.
**Example:** Given the urgency of your situation, I will look for the earliest available appointment for you today.

### Gather Appointment Preferences
**When:** After gathering necessary patient and insurance information, or when scheduling an appointment.
**Action:** Ask for preferred days and times for an appointment.
**Example:** What days and times work best for your appointment?

### Provide Earliest Opening
**When:** When the customer asks for the earliest available appointment.
**Action:** Provide the date and time of the earliest opening.
**Example:** The earliest opening we have is on [Date] at [Time].

### Offer Specific Availability
**When:** Patient provides preferred days/times, or agent has gathered preferences and checked openings.
**Action:** Check the schedule and present specific date and time options to the patient.
**Example:** Based on your availability, I have openings on Tuesday, October 24th at 10 AM or Wednesday, October 25th at 2 PM. Which works best for you?

### Confirm Selected Appointment Details
**When:** Patient has selected a preferred appointment date and time.
**Action:** Explicitly confirm the selected date and time with the patient.
**Example:** Just to confirm, you'd like to book the appointment for Tuesday, October 24th at 10 AM, correct?

### Confirm Booking Intent
**When:** After the patient has selected and confirmed a preferred appointment slot.
**Action:** Explicitly state the intent to book the chosen appointment immediately.
**Example:** Great, I'll go ahead and book that appointment for you now.

## Post-Booking Patient Details

### Collect New Patient Demographics Post-Booking
**When:** Patient is identified as new and has confirmed their appointment time.
**Action:** Request the patient's full name, date of birth, and phone number to create or update their patient record.
**Example:** Excellent. Since you're a new patient and your appointment is confirmed, could you please provide your full name, date of birth, and the best phone number for your patient record?
