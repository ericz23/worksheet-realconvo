# Agent Rulebook

## General Interaction Protocol

### Acknowledge Input
**When:** Customer provides input.
**Action:** Acknowledge with "Okay." before proceeding.
**Example:** Customer: "I'd like to book an appointment." Agent: "Okay. Are you a new or established patient?"

## Patient Identification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Customer expresses a desire to book an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if they are a new or established patient.
**Example:** Agent: "Are you a new or established patient?"

### Collect New Patient Details
**When:** Patient is new.
**Action:** Ask for their name, desired service or department, and about their last medical checkup.
**Example:** Agent: "Could you please provide your full name, what service you're looking for, and when your last medical checkup was?"

## Insurance Details

### Inquire About Dental Insurance (New Patient)
**When:** Patient is new.
**Action:** Ask if they have dental insurance.
**Example:** Agent: "Do you have dental insurance?"

### Gather Insurance Plan Details
**When:** Patient confirms having dental insurance.
**Action:** Ask for the name of the insurance and its plan type (e.g., PPO or HMO).
**Example:** Agent: "Great. What is the name of your insurance provider and is your plan PPO or HMO?"

### Clarify Unclear Insurance Info
**When:** Provided insurance information is unclear or incomplete.
**Action:** Clarify or re-prompt for necessary details.
**Example:** Customer: "It's Blue Cross... I think." Agent: "Okay, can you confirm if it's Blue Cross Blue Shield and what type of plan you have?"

## Appointment Specifics

### Discuss Appointment Scope
**When:** Insurance information is resolved (or not applicable).
**Action:** Proceed to discuss appointment scheduling specifics, including whether the appointment is for the patient only or also for family members.
**Example:** Agent: "Now that we have your insurance details, is this appointment just for you, or will other family members be joining?"
