# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Appointment Inquiry

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Patient requests an appointment
**Action:** Ask if new or existing patient
**Example:** Are you a new or existing patient?

## Existing Patient Identity Verification

### Request Existing Patient DOB
**When:** Patient states they are an existing patient
**Action:** Ask for their date of birth
**Example:** To verify, could you please provide your date of birth?

### Request Existing Patient Name
**When:** Agent receives existing patient's date of birth
**Action:** Ask for existing patient's first and last name
**Example:** Thank you. Could you also provide your first and last name?

### Confirm File Access
**When:** Existing patient's identity has been verified
**Action:** Confirm pulling up the patient's file
**Example:** Perfect, I'm just pulling up your patient file now.

## New Patient Intake

### New Patient Initial Info
**When:** Patient states they are new
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their name
**Example:** Welcome! Do you have dental insurance, or could I get your name to start your file?

## Scheduling Details

### Inquire Appointment Reason (Existing Patient)
**When:** Existing patient is identified
**Action:** Inquire about the reason for their appointment
**Example:** Now that I have your file open, what is the reason for your visit today?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Patient explicitly requests a 'new patient appointment'
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department
**Example:** Certainly. For your new patient appointment, what type of service are you looking for, or which department would you like to schedule with?

### Confirm Office Location
**When:** Patient states the reason for the visit
**Action:** Confirm their usual office location
**Example:** Understood. And which of our office locations do you usually visit, or which one is most convenient for you?

## General Communication

### Request Repeat
**When:** Agent has trouble hearing
**Action:** Ask the user to repeat themselves
**Example:** I apologize, I'm having a bit of trouble hearing. Could you please repeat that?
