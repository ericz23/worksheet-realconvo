# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Appointment Screening

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Patient requests an appointment.
**Action:** Ask if new or established patient.
**Example:** Are you a new or established patient?

## New Patient Onboarding

### New Patient Initial Info
**When:** Patient states they are new.
**Action:** Ask for dental insurance details or full name.
**Example:** Great! Do you have dental insurance, or could I get your full name please?

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** Patient explicitly requests a 'new patient appointment'.
**Action:** Ask for desired service or department.
**Example:** Certainly, for your new patient appointment, what service are you looking for, or which department would you like to visit?

## Established Patient Verification

### Identify Established Patient
**When:** Patient states they are established.
**Action:** Request date of birth and full name for identification.
**Example:** Okay, to find your records, could you please provide your full name and date of birth?

### Look Up Patient Records
**When:** Identifying information (name and DOB) collected.
**Action:** Look up patient records in the system.
**Example:** Thank you, please give me a moment while I pull up your information.

## Appointment Purpose Inquiry

### Ascertain Appointment Reason
**When:** Patient identity is confirmed.
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the appointment.
**Example:** Now that we've confirmed your identity, what is the reason for your visit today?

## Communication Protocol

### Request Repetition
**When:** Agent has trouble hearing the user.
**Action:** Ask the user to repeat what they said.
**Example:** I apologize, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please repeat what you just said?
