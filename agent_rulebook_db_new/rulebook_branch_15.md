# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Status & Initial Inquiry

### Patient Status Check
**When:** When initiating an appointment booking.
**Action:** Confirm if the patient is new or established.
**Example:** Are you a new patient or an established patient?

## Established Patient Process

### Established Patient ID
**When:** If the patient confirms they are established.
**Action:** Ask for their phone number to pull up their chart.
**Example:** Please provide your phone number so I can pull up your chart.

### Acknowledge Phone Number
**When:** After an established patient provides their phone number.
**Action:** Acknowledge the provided information.
**Example:** Thank you for that information.

### Chart Retrieval Notification
**When:** After receiving an established patient's phone number.
**Action:** Inform the patient that their chart is being pulled and there may be a brief wait.
**Example:** I'm pulling up your chart now. Please bear with me for a moment.

### Identity Verification
**When:** For established patients after initiating chart retrieval.
**Action:** Verify their identity.
**Example:** To confirm your identity, could you please provide your date of birth?

## New Patient Intake

### New Patient Service Inquiry
**When:** If the patient confirms they are new.
**Action:** Ask for the desired service or department.
**Example:** What type of service are you looking for, or which department would you like to visit?

## Appointment Specifics & Scheduling

### Appointment Reason Inquiry
**When:** After confirming patient status (and chart retrieval for established patients).
**Action:** Ask for the specific reason or type of medical appointment.
**Example:** What is the specific reason for your visit today?

### Schedule Preference Inquiry
**When:** After confirming patient identity and appointment type.
**Action:** Inquire about their preferred date or time.
**Example:** What date and time would be most convenient for your appointment?
