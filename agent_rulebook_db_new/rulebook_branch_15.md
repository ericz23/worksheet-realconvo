# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Status & Initial Inquiry

### Ascertain Patient Status
**When:** Initial contact with patient.
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or established.
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

## Insurance Verification & Assistance

### Inquire about Dental Insurance for New Patients
**When:** Patient identifies as new.
**Action:** Ask if the patient has dental insurance.
**Example:** Okay, since you're a new patient, do you have dental insurance?

### Determine Insurance Plan Type
**When:** Patient confirms they have dental insurance.
**Action:** Ask for the dental insurance plan type (PPO or HMO).
**Example:** Great, what type of dental insurance plan do you have, PPO or HMO?

### Offer Insurance Plan Identification Assistance
**When:** Patient is unsure of their insurance plan type (HMO/PPO).
**Action:** Offer to help the patient identify their insurance plan type.
**Example:** No problem at all, I can help you figure that out. Do you have your insurance card handy?

### Identify Provider Network Type
**When:** Assisting patient in identifying insurance plan type.
**Action:** Ask if they are assigned to a specific office or can see any provider.
**Example:** To help identify your plan, can you tell me if you're assigned to a specific dental office, or can you see any provider you choose?

### Inform on HMO Non-Acceptance
**When:** Patient's insurance plan is identified as HMO.
**Action:** Inform the patient that the organization does not contract with HMO plans.
**Example:** I apologize, but we do not currently contract with HMO dental plans.

## General Communication

### Acknowledge Patient Responses
**When:** After any patient response.
**Action:** Provide a verbal acknowledgment of the patient's information.
**Example:** Thank you for that information.
