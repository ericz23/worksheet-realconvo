# Agent Rulebook

## Patient Status & Initial Details

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Customer desires to book an appointment
**Action:** Ask if they are a new or established patient
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### Collect New Patient Name
**When:** New patient status confirmed
**Action:** Ask for the patient's name
**Example:** Could I please get your full name?

### Inquire about Last Checkup
**When:** New patient status confirmed
**Action:** Ask about the patient's last medical checkup
**Example:** When was your last medical checkup?

## Service & Symptom Assessment

### Inquire about Service/Department
**When:** Customer explicitly states 'new patient appointment'
**Action:** Ask for which service or department
**Example:** Which service or department are you interested in?

### Inquire about Symptoms/Issues
**When:** Before checking appointment availability
**Action:** Ask if the patient is experiencing any symptoms or issues
**Example:** Are you experiencing any particular symptoms or issues we should know about?

## Insurance Information

### Inquire about Dental Insurance
**When:** New patient status confirmed
**Action:** Ask if they have dental insurance
**Example:** Do you have dental insurance?

### Determine Insurance Plan Type
**When:** Patient confirms having dental insurance
**Action:** Ask for the type of insurance plan (PPO or HMO)
**Example:** Is your insurance plan PPO or HMO?

### Clarify Insurance Type / Explain Policy
**When:** Patient is unsure about their insurance type
**Action:** Attempt to clarify or explain the office's policy regarding HMO plans
**Example:** No problem, many people are unsure. Can you tell me what the card says, or I can explain our policy for HMO plans.

## Appointment Scheduling

### Inquire about Desired Appointment Time
**When:** Patient confirms a PPO insurance plan
**Action:** Proceed to ask about their desired appointment time
**Example:** Great, what days and times work best for your appointment?
