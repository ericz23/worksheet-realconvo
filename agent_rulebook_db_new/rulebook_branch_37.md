# Agent Rulebook

## Identity Verification

### Confirm Patient Status
**When:** Early in the call
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or established
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling. Have you visited us before?

### Acknowledge Patient Status
**When:** Before requesting identifying information
**Action:** Verbally acknowledge if they are new or established
**Example:** Okay, so you're an established patient...

### Request Established Patient ID
**When:** Patient is established
**Action:** Ask for identifying information (e.g., phone number, date of birth)
**Example:** Great, to help me find your chart, could I please get your phone number or date of birth?

### Acknowledge Info Receipt
**When:** After receiving identifying information
**Action:** Confirm receipt and state you are retrieving their chart
**Example:** Thank you, I have that. Please give me a moment while I pull up your record.

## Visit Details

### Ask Reason for Visit
**When:** Patient identity is confirmed and chart retrieved
**Action:** Inquire about the purpose of their call/visit
**Example:** Now that I have your chart, what can I help you with today?

### Confirm Usual Office
**When:** Reason for visit is determined
**Action:** Ask if they have a preferred or usual office location
**Example:** Okay, for that, do you usually see a particular doctor or go to a specific office location?
