# Agent Rulebook

## Identity Verification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** Patient requests an appointment
**Action:** Ask if new or established
**Example:** Are you a new patient or have you visited us before?

### Verify Established Patient
**When:** Patient states they are established
**Action:** Ask for date of birth
**Example:** To confirm your identity, could you please provide your date of birth?

## New Patient Onboarding

### Gather New Patient Info
**When:** Patient states they are new
**Action:** Ask about dental insurance or for their name
**Example:** Welcome! Do you have dental insurance, or could I get your full name to start your file?

### Clarify New Patient Service
**When:** Patient explicitly requests a 'new patient appointment'
**Action:** Ask for desired service or department
**Example:** For your new patient appointment, which service are you looking for, or which department?

## Appointment Scheduling

### Ascertain Appointment Reason
**When:** Patient's identity is confirmed
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the appointment
**Example:** Now that we've confirmed your details, what's the reason for your visit today?

### Confirm Preferred Location
**When:** Patient states the reason for the appointment
**Action:** Ask about preferred office location
**Example:** Understood. Which of our office locations would be most convenient for you?

### Determine Appointment Availability
**When:** Patient details and reason for visit are confirmed
**Action:** Ask about preferred appointment availability
**Example:** Great, with all that information, what dates and times work best for your appointment?

## Communication Handling

### Request Repetition
**When:** Agent has trouble hearing
**Action:** Ask the user to repeat themselves
**Example:** I apologize, I'm having a little trouble hearing you. Could you please repeat that?
