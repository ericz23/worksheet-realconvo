# Agent Rulebook

## Initial Contact

### Greet and Identify
**When:** Call starts
**Action:** Greet the caller, identify the organization, and offer assistance.
**Example:** Hello, thank you for calling [Dental Office Name]. How may I help you today?

### Handle Confusion
**When:** Customer expresses confusion or asks for clarification
**Action:** Repeat or clarify the previous question.
**Example:** I apologize, let me rephrase that. I was asking, do you have dental insurance?

## Patient Identification

### Determine Patient Status
**When:** After initial greeting
**Action:** Ask if the patient is new or existing.
**Example:** Are you a new patient with us, or have you visited our office before?

## Insurance Information

### New Patient Insurance Inquiry
**When:** Patient indicates they are new
**Action:** Inquire about dental insurance.
**Example:** Do you have dental insurance you'll be using for your visit?

### Obtain Insurance Provider Name
**When:** Patient indicates having dental insurance
**Action:** Ask for the name of their dental insurance provider.
**Example:** Great, what is the name of your dental insurance company?

### Confirm Insurance and Plan Type
**When:** Insurance provider name is provided
**Action:** Confirm the insurance name and ask for the plan type (e.g., PPO, HMO).
**Example:** Okay, [Insurance Name]. Is that typically a PPO or an HMO plan?

### Verify In-network Status
**When:** Insurance details (provider and plan type) are provided
**Action:** Check if the office is in-network with the provided insurance plan.
**Example:** Let me quickly check if we are in-network with your [Insurance Name] [Plan Type] plan.

## Appointment Details

### Determine Appointment Scope
**When:** After patient identification and initial insurance check
**Action:** Ask if the appointment is for the customer only or also for family members.
**Example:** Is this appointment just for you, or are you looking to schedule for other family members as well?

### Ascertain Visit Reason
**When:** After confirming patient status and appointment scope
**Action:** Ask for the reason for the visit.
**Example:** What is the primary reason for your visit today?

## Scheduling

### Inquire About Availability
**When:** Patient, insurance, and appointment scope details confirmed
**Action:** Inquire about the customer's preferred scheduling availability.
**Example:** Thank you for all that information. What days or times work best for you to come in for your appointment?
